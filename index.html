Miguel Cardoso Oliveira

I am an Economics and Finance Ph.D. student at Nova School of Business and Economics. My primary research interests are in corporate finance, more specifically in bankruptcy and financial distress. My work is supervised by Fernando Anjos and Miguel Ferreira, and is funded by the FCT Ph.D. Scholarship.

Before joining the Ph.D. program I worked as a financial consultant for companies going through reorganization and bankruptcy.

I will join the Katz School of Business, University of Pittsburgh, as an Assistant Professor of Finance in August, 2025.
